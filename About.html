About The Photographer

I'm Aries Jade Ambrocio, also known as AJ Pampanga. I am a freelance photographer based in Pampanga. I am also a teacher/coach in Pampanga State Agricultural University. You'll be able to see me around the university, mostly going in and out of the multipurpose center.

My passion for photography goes beyond just taking pictures; it's about creating art that resonates with the soul. Each image I capture is a reflection of my dedication to my craft and my commitment to capturing life's most precious moments.

Thank you for visiting my website. I look forward to the opportunity to work with you and capture the moments that matter most.
